Silver Lake Trail to Solitude Lake Trail

Directions:

Follow Big Cottonwood Canyon Road (7000 South Wasatch Blvd.) about 14.2 miles up the canyon to the Silver Lake Visitor's Center parking area across from the Brighton Store on the west side of the road.

Park here or in the overflow parking to the northeast of the visitor center.

The last time we went, it was $5 per person to use the facilities. We have our own snowshoes, but they will gladly rent you a pair.

Trail Description:

The Trail walks around Silver Lake and gives you access to Solitude Lake and Twin Lakes. No Swimming is aloud at Silver Lake.

From the northern side of the lake a trail leads up 1.5 miles to Lake Solitude.

Distance:

3.5 mile roundtrip

Elevation Gain:

940 ft

Trail Rating:

Easy around Silver Lake, then I would call it moderate to get to Lake Solitude.

Known Avalanche Locations:

I have not heard of any. But this is backcountry, so use caution.

Parking Spaces:

Yes

Restroom Facilities:

Yes
