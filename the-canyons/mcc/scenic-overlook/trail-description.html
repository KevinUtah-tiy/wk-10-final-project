Desolation Trail to Scenic Overlook

Directions:

Desolation Trailhead sits 2.6 miles up Mill Creek Canyon from the fee station â€“ just past the Mill Creek Inn on the south side of the road.

Trail Description:

From the restroom facilities, take the trail west. It makes a sharp turn to the south and increases in elevation quickly. This elevation gain isn't very long (approximately .1 mile), just far enough to get a good warm up. The trail heads west and makes 5 switch-backs to take the hiker south as you make it up the east side of the mountain. Another switch-back will take you to the west side of the mountain. You will make your way south, then east, then south again. You will traverse five more switch-backs before reaching the trail exit to get to the scenic overlook.

Return on the same path.

Distance:

About 5 miles roundtrip

Elevation Gain:

Elevation at trailhead: 5820 ft
Elevation at the Scenic Overlook: 7045 ft
Gain: 1225 ft
