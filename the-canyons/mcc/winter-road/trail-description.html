Winter Road to the Big Water Yurt

Directions:

Maple Grove Picnic area sits five miles up Mill Creek canyon. Parking is available at the gate. No motorized access allowed beyond the winter gate. From the winter gate,

Trail Description:

From the Maple Grove Picnic area, Millcreek Canyon Road is easily hiked, skied or snowshoed. I try to stay out of the groomed ski tracks as there is snow on the sides of the road. The road is maintained for winter pleasure, too.

Return on the same path.

Distance:

About 9 miles roundtrip

Elevation Gain:

Elevation at trailhead: 6110 ft
Elevation at the Yurt: 7650 ft
Gain: 1540 ft

Trail Rating:

Moderate because of the length. You may turn around at any point, though.

Known Avalanche Locations:

Low.

Parking Spaces:

Yes

Restroom Facilities:

Yes
